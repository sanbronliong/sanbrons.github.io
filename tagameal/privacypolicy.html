PRIVACY POLICY

This Privacy Policy explains how VFood360 Station Sdn Bhd(here in after referred to as "vfood"), the company of http://www.tagameal.com, uses your personal information which you provided to VFood when interacting with VFood or by using our service, including but not limited to our VFood website http://www.tagameal.com (“Website”) and mobile applications (apps). In this respect, you shall be responsible for providing accurate, not misleading, complete and up-to-date personal information to VFood and consent to the processing of these personal information by VFood in Malaysia and outside Malaysia. 

In the event of any conflict between the English and any other language versionin the website, the English version shall prevail. VFood shall have the right to modify, update or amend the terms of this Privacy Policy at any time by placing the updated Privacy Policy on the Website. By continuing to interact with VFood or by continuing to use VFood’s services following the modifications, updates or amendments to this Privacy Policy shall signify your acceptance of such modifications, updates or amendments.

 “Personal Information” refers to personal data which you have provided to VFood and any information that can be used to identify you.

 “Processing” refers to collecting, holding, storing, recording, organizing, adapting, using, disclosing or correcting Personal Information.



What Personal Information VFood collects from you?

Food collects Personal Information from you when you register with VFood, when you order goods or services from VFood or use the Website such as but not limited to your name, address, telephone number, NRIC/passport no., email address and credit/debit card details. VFood also collects Personal Information when you complete any customer survey. Website usage information may also be collected using cookies. 

The provision of your Personal Information is voluntary. However, in the event you do not provide the required Personal Information, VFood may not be able to communicate with you and/or to provide you with the services requested.



Cookies and Google Analytics

To facilitate and customize your experience with the Website, we store cookies on your computer. A cookie is a small text file that is stored on a User's computer for record-keeping purposes which contains information about that User. We use cookies to save you time while using the Website, remind us who you are, and track and target User interests in order to provide a customized experience. Cookies also allow us to collect Non-Personally Identifiable Information from you, like which pages you visited and what links you clicked on. Use of this information helps us to create a more user-friendly experience for all visitors.

This Website uses Google Analytics, a web analytics service provided by Google, Inc. ("Google"). Google Analytics uses "cookies", which are text files saved on your computer, to help the Website analyze how you use the site. Google will use this information for the purpose of evaluating your use of the Website, compiling reports on Website activity for Website operators and providing other services relating to Website activity and internet usage.

Google will not associate your IP address with any other data held by Google.

In addition, we may use Third Party Advertising Companies to display advertisements on our Website. As part of their service, they may place separate cookies on your computer. We have no access to or control over these cookies. This Privacy Policy covers the use of cookies by our Website only and does not cover the use of cookies by any Advertiser. Most browsers automatically accept cookies, but you may be able to modify your browser settings to decline cookies. Please note that if you decline or delete these cookies, some parts of the Website may not work properly.



How will VFood use the Personal Information collected from you?

Personal Information collected by VFood is used to process your order and to manage your account. VFood may also use your Personal Information to email you about other products or services that VFood thinks may be of interest to you and to comply with its obligations under applicable laws, where required.



Transfer and disclosure of Personal Information

As VFood’s storage facilities and servers may be located in other jurisdictions; your Personal Information may be transferred to, stored, used and processed in a jurisdiction other than Malaysia.
From time to time VFood may send your Personal Information to third parties which VFood considers may have goods or services which are of interest to you. In processing your order, VFood may send your Personal Information to credit reference and fraud prevention agencies where permitted, under applicable laws. If you do not wish to be contacted by third parties please email to VFood360’s Data Compliance Officers at support@tagameal.com



Access to your Personal Information and rectification requests

Subject to any exceptions under applicable laws, you have the right to request a copy of the Personal Information and rectify the Personal Information VFood holds on you at any time. Please email to VFood’s Data Compliance Officer if you would like to receive a copy of this information, to submit a Personal Information rectification request or if you seek further information on this Privacy Policy – support@tagameal.com . There will be a small charge for processing this Personal Information access request. 



Retention of your Personal Information

VFood will retain your Personal Information for the duration necessary to fulfill the foregoing purposes and until you delete your VFood account.



Other Websites

Our website may have links to other websites. This Privacy Policy only applies to this Website. You should therefore read the privacy policies of the other websites when you are using those sites.
