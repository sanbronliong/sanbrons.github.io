Frequently Asked Questions

What is Betform?


Betform is a decentralized social betting platform that brings funs, privacy, and safety for funds to players. Our main operations are governed by smart contracts.

How many games do you have?


We will only be providing three well-crafted and highly visual games after the token sale. The three games are Texas Poker, Big 2 and In-Between. We will continue to develop and introduce more multiplayers games after the token sale.

What language options are available?


We only have English version at launch. We will be looking at introducing support for other languages when our platform grows.

How to place a bet?


You are required to be a registred Betform client. If you have not signed in yet, you can do that on our Registration page. The first thing to do after completing registration is to log in by typing your username and password in a bar which is located in the upper part of the page.

How are you intending to deal with the volatility in the Betform coin price?


We couldn’t control the price of Betform coins in the exchanges. But we will update the average latest price of Betform coins in the website and App. Players would have to consider their bets accordingly based on the latest price per Betform coin.

When can Betform goes live?


We will officially launch our games and website after the token sale and we obtained the operating license.

Is my account safe with Betform?


Betform grants a lot of attention to security of client information which guarantees the safety of account data.
